CLUB HISTORY
Our story since 2000
2000
Founded — 2000
Sevana Arts & Sports Club was registered (REG 953/2000) by passionate sports enthusiasts in Padinarepadam, Thirurkad. The club was built on the values of sportsmanship, community, and the love of football.
2003
First Tournament Victory
Sevana won its first major local tournament, defeating rival clubs in the Thirurkad Panchayat League. The community celebrated this historic achievement with great pride.
2008
Cricket & Arts Expansion
The club expanded to include cricket, indoor games, and cultural arts programs. The iconic logo — featuring a football, cricket bat, and veena — was adopted to reflect this multi-disciplinary spirit.
2015
Inter-Panchayat Championship
Sevana emerged as champions of the inter-panchayat football championship, beating 16 clubs. This remains one of the most celebrated achievements in club history.
2025
Sevana Premier League
A landmark moment — Sevana launches its first fully organised 8-team Premier League with digital tracking, professional management, and the first-ever Ballon d'Or award. A new era begins!
